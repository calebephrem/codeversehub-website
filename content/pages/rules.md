> These rules exist to protect every member and ensure CodeVerse Hub remains a friendly, welcoming space. Please read them carefully — ignorance is not an excuse.

## Follow Discord Terms & Guidelines

All server rules are based on Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. Modded clients like Vencord or BetterDiscord are allowed.

---

## No Raids, Doxxing, or Swatting

Do not participate in, joke about, or threaten raids, doxxing, or swatting. Any such threat will result in a **permanent ban**.

- Sharing information related to these actions is also prohibited, even "for awareness"
- You may discuss incidents for awareness only, without encouraging the behavior

---

## No Scams, Malware, or Malicious Links

Using Discord for scams, malware, viruses, IP grabbers, or harmful links is **strictly prohibited** — both inside the server and in DMs.

- Cybersecurity discussions are allowed only in appropriate channels
- Do not share malware, source code, tutorials, tools, or stream such content

---

## No Sharing Personal Information

Leaking or sharing personal information is not allowed. This includes:

- IP addresses, phone numbers, and physical addresses
- Legal names, IDs, or similar identifying data

---

## Voice Channel Conduct

Prohibited behavior in voice channels includes:

- Racism, sexism, hate speech, or ToS violations
- Earrape or disruptive behavior
- Recording or archiving conversations without owner permission

---

## No NSFW, Gore, or Shock Content

NSFW content, gore, shock content, or explicit behavior is strictly prohibited. This includes jokes or indirect references to such content. Racism and sexism are also not allowed.

---

## No Politics or Religious Debates

Politics and religion are not topics for this server. Any offensive content toward countries, institutions, or beliefs is prohibited.

> Any reference to Nazis/Hitler or equivalents will result in a **permanent ban**.

---

## Profiles Must Follow Rules

Your Discord profile must comply with server rules and Discord ToS. The following can be moderated:

- Names, bios, avatars, pronouns, and statuses
- NSFW content, hate speech, impersonation, or malicious content

---

## No Advertising

Advertising is not allowed on this server.

- DM advertising without a valid reason is prohibited and can result in a ban
- If someone asks for a link, share it via DM only
- Projects may be shared only in the appropriate channels

---

## No Rule Loopholes — Use Common Sense

These rules are not exhaustive. Trying to bypass rules is not allowed. If you're unsure, ask staff via tickets or just don't do it.

---

## Respect Moderation Decisions

Do not disrupt the server if you disagree with a punishment. Appeal respectfully through proper channels.

Ban appeals can be submitted via `/ban-appeal` (recommended) or through ModMail if you cannot access the website.

If bias is suspected after appeal, contact staff in this order via tickets/modmail only:

1. Head Mod
2. Admin
3. Owner

> Do not DM staff or add them without consent.

---

## TL;DR

> Use common sense, respect others, and act maturely. That's really all it takes.
